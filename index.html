FRIENDS

I first watched this during the start of pandemic. Me, my sister, and my mom, would watch 2 episodes every night after dinner. I loved it so much because every episode always had me laughing. It was incredibly sad for me when we reached the last episode. I love the sitcom so much I still rewatch it to this day.

The Office

I started watching The Office at the summer of 2025. This was the first every mockumentary show I have watched and I fell in love with it quickly. I love all the pranks and humor in each episode and I enjoyed it so much I finished the whole thing in 2 and a half weeks! I remember crying for 2 hours while and after watching the last episode, because of how much the show inspired and imapcted me. I have to admit, it might be my most favorite ending of all time. I miss watching it so much. It reminds me of how happy life was during 8th grade and how I took the best school year of my life for granted. (S.Y 24-25)
